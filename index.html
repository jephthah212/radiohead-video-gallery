Nude

This version of 'Nude’ was part of Radiohead's 'In Rainbows - From the Basement' performance which was released in July 2008.

‘Nude’ was the penultimate song of their song and was originally from their 2007 album ‘In Rainbows’.

‘Nude’ was first recorded during sessions for their third album ‘OK Computer’ (1997). They played it during live performances and soon became one of their best-known unreleased tracks.
